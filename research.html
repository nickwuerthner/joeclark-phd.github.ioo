Research

As an academic, part of my time is devoted to trying to develop new knowledge via research. Some of my work deals with the logic of decision-making under conditions of uncertainty, a topic I have researched with computer simulation methods going back to my PhD dissertation. More recently, I've become very interested in how data and analytics can be turned into practical value for small businesses. I continue to explore that area through interviews and case studies, with more research planned in the near future.
